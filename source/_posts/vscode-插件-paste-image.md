**paste-image 插件**

配置

```json
"pasteImage.filePathConfirmInputBoxMode": "onlyName",
"pasteImage.path": "${projectRoot}/source/images/",
"pasteImage.basePath": "${projectRoot}/source",
"pasteImage.forceUnixStyleSeparator": true,
"pasteImage.prefix": "/"
```
